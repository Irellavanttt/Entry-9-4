BdoubleO100

Bdoubleo100, also known as Bdubs, mentions traditional art in reference to his works constantly. Whether it's by sketching, composition, colour theory, or line weight, he is actively thinking about all these elements when composing his builds. With his Hermitcraft Season 10 Nether Hub, he created these linear frames on each diagonal angle to try and help with the navigation of the Hub. He used glass and walls to create linear weight in a 3D space.

Bdubs also came out with a video titled “Can I Build...What I Draw?” In this video, Bdubs takes traditional arts and Minecraft to a whole new level. He sketched a drawing of a small cabin in the woods and looked for ways to recreate the feeling of a painting within Minecraft. Using the program Axuim, which is a program to assist with large-scale build layouts, he ended up creating a masterpiece of a portrait within a 3D space.
